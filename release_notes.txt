## JellyRip v1.0.6 Highlights

### Preview and selection
- Added per-title Preview support directly in the title picker.
- Preview runs as a short disposable sample rip and can auto-open in VLC.

### Real workflow resume
- Resume now restores metadata context (title, year, season, selected titles, episode fields).
- Input prompts can prefill saved values from interrupted sessions.

### Reliability policy enforcement
- Rip outcomes are normalized to all-or-nothing success.
- Invalid, missing, aborted, or failed-title outputs are treated as session failure.
- Failed sessions now wipe output media while preserving metadata for resume.

### Settings and naming
- Added fallback naming mode options: timestamp, auto title, and auto title + timestamp.
- Settings UI reorganized into clearer tabs for everyday and advanced controls.

Full changelog: https://github.com/unexpear/JellyRip/blob/main/CHANGELOG.md
